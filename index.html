Meet Your Doctor

Dr. John C. Goldsworthy

Since establishing my practice in 1980, I have been a pillar of health and wellness in the Chicago community. With over four decades of clinical experience, I understand that no two bodies are alike. My approach is highly flexible—I take the time to listen to your concerns and adapt treatments to fit your specific lifestyle, pain levels, and recovery goals.

Comprehensive Care

Expertise Across All Modalities

I treat a vast array of conditions, from everyday back and neck pain to complex sports injuries. My clinic focuses on modern, evidence-based chiropractic adjustments and rehabilitation.

General Chiropractic Care

Expert diagnosis and flexible treatment plans for a wide variety of conditions, including sciatica, migraines, neck stiffness, and chronic back pain. I adapt my techniques (from gentle mobilization to standard adjustments) to suit your comfort level.

Sports Chiropractic Focus

Drawing on specialized post-graduate training as a Chiropractic Sports Physician, I specialize in the assessment, management, and rehabilitation of sports-related injuries. Perfect for athletes looking to optimize biomechanics and speed up recovery times.

Physical Therapy & Rehab

Beyond the adjustment, I provide comprehensive physical therapy rehabilitation. By strengthening supporting muscles and improving flexibility, I address the root cause of your pain to prevent future injuries.

On-Site Diagnostics

Convenient On-Site X-Ray

Why wait days for imaging results or travel to a secondary facility? I make diagnosis straightforward by offering reliable, professional X-ray imaging right here in my office.

  • Clear answers regarding bone health and alignment during your visit.
  • Safer, more precise adjustments based on real, immediate imaging.
  • Highly convenient—saving you time, travel, and extra appointments.
